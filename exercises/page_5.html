Ruby the Jack Russell

Introduction

Where it all began, Ruby was born in 2009 on a lovely summer's morning in August. She had lots of siblings to play with before she came to her ever home in Galway.

Her home, Galway

Ruby has worked very hard over the last few years, guarding the local park. She spent her time also navigating the country roads and calling to her neighbouring canine friends in her spare time.

Her Favourite Things

Ruby enjoys a variety of activites from playing ball to jumping in the sea after a long day at work. Ruby has a fondness for a great sleep too!
